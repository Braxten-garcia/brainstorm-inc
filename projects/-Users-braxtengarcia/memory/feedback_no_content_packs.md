---
name: Don't call packs "Content Packs"
description: Avoid the term "Content Packs" in external copy — focus on platform-driven readiness and usage, name the specific tools
type: feedback
---

Never refer to BrainStorm's packs as "Content Packs" in external-facing copy.

**Why:** It oversimplifies and devalues the product. BrainStorm doesn't want to be confused with a Content Services Provider or a Content Library. The focus should be on how the platform drives readiness and actual usage of specific tools.

**How to apply:** Instead of "Content Packs," name the specific tool (e.g., M365 Copilot, AI Security) and frame around readiness, adoption, and usage. Lead with the platform outcome, not the content format.
